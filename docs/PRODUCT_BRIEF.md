# ProofSnap Product Brief

## One-sentence pitch
ProofSnap helps service workers create timestamped photo proof reports and export professional PDFs in seconds.

## Primary customer
Start with one narrow customer segment:

**Airbnb cleaners and small property managers** who need before/after cleaning proof, damage documentation, and client-ready reports.

Secondary segments:

- Contractors and handymen
- Landlords
- Tenants documenting move-in/move-out condition
- Mobile mechanics
- Inspectors
- Delivery/installation workers

## Pain points
- Clients dispute whether work was completed.
- Property condition is hard to prove after the fact.
- Workers send messy photo dumps over text.
- Small operators cannot justify expensive field-service software.
- People need documentation that looks professional but takes very little time.

## MVP positioning
**Photo reports for jobs, rentals, and repairs.**

## MVP value proposition
- Take or import photos.
- Add notes and timestamps.
- Optional location stamp.
- Generate a polished PDF.
- Share it with clients, owners, guests, or records.

## Product principles
- Local-first.
- No account required.
- Fast enough to use at a job site.
- Clear enough for non-technical workers.
- Boring, reliable, and professional.

## Free vs Pro
### Free
- 3 reports/month
- Watermark on PDFs
- Basic templates

### Pro
- Unlimited reports
- Remove watermark
- Add company logo/contact info
- Save reusable branding
- Export higher-quality PDFs

## Suggested pricing
- $4.99/month
- $29.99/year
- $19.99-$39.99 lifetime unlock

For a solo side project, start with annual + lifetime unlock. Add monthly later only if users ask for it.

## Competitor-informed positioning
- Match the essentials users expect from Timemark: timestamps, optional GPS stamps, project/report organization, notes, cloud-backup readiness, and watermark removal in Pro.
- Preserve the offline reliability of Cleaning Proof Photo Reports: reports, photos, and PDFs should work without an account or network connection.
- Keep the simple watermark/pro unlock mental model users know from timestamp-camera apps, but differentiate with professional report organization and PDF export.
- Treat bulk capture, ghost/guide overlays, reusable labels, and team workflows as post-MVP differentiators rather than core launch blockers.
