# rampready Product Brief

## One-sentence pitch
rampready helps people new to boating decide whether a ramp and launch window look approachable before they tow to the water.

## Primary customer
Start with one narrow customer segment:

**New trailer boaters in Florida/Tampa Bay** who are learning how to choose ramps, interpret weather and tide conditions, and avoid stressful first launches.

Secondary segments:

- Occasional boaters who want a quick pre-launch check.
- Families trailering small boats or personal watercraft.
- Boat-rental and boating-course customers planning their first solo launch.
- Local boating clubs helping beginners learn safe planning habits.

## Pain points
- New boaters do not know which ramps are beginner-friendly.
- Weather, tide, water level, current, and alert data are scattered across official sources.
- It is hard to know whether conditions are merely imperfect or genuinely risky.
- Popular ramps may have local hazards, shallow water, or setup complexity that beginners miss.
- New boaters need plain-language guidance, not raw marine data alone.

## MVP positioning
**Beginner-friendly boat ramp and launch-window planning.**

## MVP value proposition
- Search launch ramps in the target region.
- Save favorite ramps.
- See simple green/yellow/red launch-window guidance.
- Understand top reasons behind each score.
- Tune thresholds for boat type, wind, gusts, waves, tides, and daylight-only comfort.
- Report ramp issues for admin review.

## Product principles
- Planning-only, never navigation or emergency guidance.
- Explain conditions in plain language for beginners.
- Prefer official sources and source transparency.
- Keep confidence visible when data is missing or stale.
- Prioritize safety, humility, and clear disclaimers over false certainty.

## Free vs Pro
### Free
- Save a small number of ramps.
- Basic launch-window guidance.
- Standard beginner thresholds.

### Pro
- More saved ramps.
- Advanced preference profiles.
- Regional alerts and richer planning reminders.
- Future offline/cached planning packs.

## Suggested pricing
- $19.99/year.
- $29.99-$49.99 lifetime unlock.

Start with annual + lifetime unlock after the core planning flow is reliable.
