# Additional Low-Maintenance App Ideas

These are ranked for a solo mobile developer who wants side income without constant content updates, moderation, or backend maintenance.

## 1. Warranty Vault Lite
A local-first receipt, serial number, and warranty organizer.

### Why it can work
People lose receipts and warranty details. A simple app that stores product photos, receipt photos, warranty length, serial numbers, and reminders is useful without requiring a backend.

### Monetization
- Free: 10 items
- Pro: unlimited items, PDF export, iCloud/Google Drive backup later
- Pricing: $9.99-$19.99 lifetime

### Maintenance level
Low.

## 2. Pet Sitter Report Cards
A simple app for pet sitters and dog walkers to send polished visit reports.

### Core flow
Add pet/client, take photos, mark food/water/walk/medicine/checklist, export/share a cute report.

### Monetization
- Free: watermark
- Pro: branding, unlimited clients, reusable templates
- Pricing: $19.99/year or $29.99 lifetime

### Maintenance level
Low to medium.

## 3. Move-In Damage Log
A tenant-focused version of ProofSnap for documenting apartment condition before move-in and after move-out.

### Core flow
Room-by-room checklist, photo evidence, notes, timestamped PDF export.

### Monetization
- Paid app: $4.99-$9.99
- Or free with one report, paid unlock for full export

### Maintenance level
Low, but be careful not to position it as legal advice.

## 4. Micro-SOP Checklist
A recurring checklist app for small service businesses where each checklist item can require photo proof.

### Example niches
- Cleaning turnovers
- Gym opening/closing checklist
- Restaurant bathroom checks
- Salon closing checklist
- Equipment inspection

### Monetization
- Free: 2 checklists
- Pro: unlimited checklists, PDF export, branding

### Maintenance level
Low, but broader than ProofSnap.

## 5. Simple Mileage + Receipt PDF
A very simple mileage and expense capture tool for independent contractors.

### Core flow
Log trip, attach receipt photos, categorize expense, export monthly PDF/CSV.

### Monetization
- Annual subscription or one-time unlock

### Maintenance level
Medium because tax-related positioning can create support questions. Avoid giving tax advice.

## 6. Kids Artwork Scanner Portfolio
Parents photograph kids' artwork, auto-crop lightly, organize by child/year, and export a yearly PDF.

### Monetization
- One-time unlock for unlimited albums/PDF export

### Maintenance level
Low.

## Best alternate pick
If ProofSnap feels too business-focused, build **Warranty Vault Lite**. It is simpler, more consumer-friendly, and can be almost entirely local. The tradeoff is that consumers are usually less willing to pay than business users.
